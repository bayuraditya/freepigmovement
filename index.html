FUNBOARD


Description

FUNBOARD This is the board you will have the most fun on during your progression as a beginner and also as intermediate surfer. It also caters for the bigger body frame that needs extra foam to keep you in the water. The board has been designed for stability, comfort and ease. The dimensions vary in relation to the user but the main focus is on VOLUME to make the board buoyant so it's easy to paddle and pick up waves. The overall shape can be designed to the user specifications so whether you would like a mal nose or a retro nose or just somewhere in between that will be your choice to make!!
